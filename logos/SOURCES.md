# Logo Sources

All files below were sourced from Wikimedia Commons, which hosts public-domain works of
U.S. federal employees (17 U.S.C. § 105). Each candidate was rejected if its Commons title,
page description, or category contained "seal," AND — going one step further — each surviving
candidate was rendered/viewed and visually compared against known seal designs already present
in this directory before being downloaded. Two candidates that passed the text-based "seal"
filter were rejected anyway after visual inspection proved they were the seal in substance
(see Rejected table below).
